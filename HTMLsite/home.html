SAY HI TO GOOP!

Welcome to Charity RPG! The game that fits donating into your budget.

As you blast through donation milestones, Goop will be right by your side every step of the way! But--! Oh no! Goop has no swag!! But worry not. You can earn very VERY stylish hats for Goop by leveling up and completing milestones.

"But how do I level up?" one may ask. Easy peasy! You know that money you already spend on eating out or unused streaming subscriptions? Consider donating a small portion of that to a charity of your choice to level up and unlock very cool hats to customize Goop with.

That's it! Now all that's left is to fill out your monthly budget and, if you donated this month, watch the hats come rolling in. Click "Budget" to get started and get that swag! \(^0^)/
